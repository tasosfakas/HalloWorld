2. Legal Theory & Anticipated Violations

(Carried forward and consolidated across the entire matter.)

  • V1 Unrechtmäßige Abmahnung (§§ 1004, 242 BGB)
    • Elements: Abmahnung must be factually correct, sufficiently specific, proportionate; if defective, employee has Entfernungsanspruch.
    • What proves it: Refutation of the alleged conduct; contradictory contemporaneous evidence; absence of prior warning; reframing by the alleged victim themselves (e.g. Joshi reclassifying the "interview" as "open discussion").
    • What defeats it: Documentary substantiation of the alleged conduct; valid prior warnings; proportionality justification.
  • V2 Maßregelungsverbot (§ 612a BGB)
    • Elements: (i) protected activity; (ii) employer knowledge; (iii) detriment; (iv) causation.
    • What proves it: Tight chronological sequence; employer-side admission of awareness; pretextual reasoning.
    • What defeats it: Non-retaliatory rationale predating the protected acts; legitimate business reason for the detriment.
  • V3 AGG-Diskriminierung (§§ 1, 3, 7 AGG; § 22 Indizien Beweislastumkehr)
    • Elements: Less favourable treatment causally linked to a protected ground (ethnic origin / nationality / establishment-location).
    • What proves it: Indizien mosaic — promoted comparators, structural barriers, manager admissions, intake-system categorisation, identifiable mechanism (interview asymmetry, program eligibility).
    • What defeats it: Justification by sachlicher Grund.
  • V4 AGG-Belästigung (§ 3 III AGG)
    • Hostile environment from totality of conduct; multiple-victim corroboration.
  • V5 Mobbing / Persönlichkeitsrechtsverletzung (Art. 1, 2 I GG i.V.m. §§ 823, 1004 BGB)
    • Systematische Verkettung of acts; BAG-Rechtsprechung requires multiple, related incidents over time.
  • V6 Fürsorgepflicht (§§ 241 II, 618 BGB)
    • Employer on notice + foreseeable health damage + no protective measure.
  • V7 Beschwerdebearbeitungspflicht (§ 13 AGG, § 84 BetrVG)
    • Duty to substantively investigate; independence requirement.
  • V8 Hinausmobben — preparatory steps to dismissal: authority denial, paper-trail-building, role-stripping, manager-side coordination.
  • V9 Fehlerhafte Leistungsbeurteilung — procedural appraisal defects (no discussion, no objective criteria, frozen ratings, false statements by appraiser).
  • V10 Defamation / Üble Nachrede / Würdeverletzung (§§ 186, 187 StGB; § 823 BGB; Art. 1, 2 I GG).
  • V11 § 36 HinSchG retaliation — Beweislastumkehr; protected internal whistleblowing reports.
  • V12 Code-of-Conduct / § 8 / § 13 HinSchG independence & confidentiality of internal channel.
